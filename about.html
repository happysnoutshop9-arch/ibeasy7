About IB Easy 7

Helping IB students worldwide navigate their assessments with clarity and confidence.

The Tutor Behind the Brand

I'm a former IB student who scored 43/45, and I've spent the past four years helping students around the world tackle their IB assessments with clarity and confidence. Over that time, I've supported more than 100 students across IA, EE, TOK, and IO — from students starting completely from scratch to those refining a final draft days before submission.

My background is in IB Economics and Business Management, though I now support written work across all subject groups. I'm currently based in Portugal and work with students globally — sessions and feedback happen entirely online, so wherever you are, we can work together.

My approach: I believe the most valuable thing I can do is help you genuinely understand your work — not hand you something to submit. Every piece of feedback I give is designed to make you a stronger writer and thinker. You are always the author of your own work.
