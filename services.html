Frequently Asked Questions

Still unsure? Here are the questions we hear most often.

Will this affect my academic integrity?

No. All our services follow a guidance and feedback model — we provide written comments, annotated suggestions, and structural advice, but we do not write or rewrite your work for you. You remain the sole author of your assessment. This is consistent with the IB's academic integrity policy, which permits students to seek feedback and editing guidance so long as the intellectual work is their own. If you are unsure, we recommend discussing with your IB coordinator before booking.

How long does it take to receive feedback?

Typically within 3–5 business days, depending on the service tier and current booking volume. Full Service clients receive ongoing support with shorter individual turnaround times for each round. If you have an urgent deadline, please contact us in advance and we will do our best to accommodate you.

Can I upgrade from Feedback Review to Full Edit later?

Yes. If you start with a Feedback Review and later decide you would like Full Edit or Full Service support, simply pay the difference between the two tiers. We will apply the work already done toward the upgraded service — there is no duplication of effort or charge.

Do you cover all IB subjects?

For IA, we cover all written IB subjects — the only exceptions are Music and Visual Arts, whose unique portfolio and creative formats fall outside our feedback model. For the EE, we support all six subject groups. TOK support covers both the Essay and the Exhibition for all prescribed titles. IO currently specialises in Chinese A, with more languages being added — please contact us to enquire about your specific language.
